# Runbook SEV-3: Volume or Distribution Anomaly

## Trigger
Volume drops below threshold or distribution shift exceeds L1 tolerance.

## Immediate Actions
1. Confirm source campaign and channel mix changes.
2. Compare anomaly against planned spend changes.
3. Flag suspicious sources for temporary quarantine.

## Recovery Steps
1. Tune anomaly thresholds only with stakeholder approval.
2. Reconcile against ad-platform and analytics exports.
3. Document incident cause in phase issues log.

## Exit Condition
Volume ratio and distribution distance remain within target for the next scheduled run.
